likes and dislikes

likes

I like games (FPS, simulation, survival games, sandbox, etc.). I also like doing art, though I'm not very good at it. I enjoy all kinds of jokes, and I especially like making jokes and engaging in back-and-forth banter.

Food-wise, I love pizza; it's the second best food in the world! I also love VR games like VRChat, but I don't play them very often. I'm fascinated by the tech world, particularly how databases get hacked. I'm curious about the methods hackers use, such as CSS code, other code, or attack plans.

dislikes

I have a strong aversion to dishonesty and manipulation. I particularly dislike those who lie or twist my words, especially when it comes to personal matters. I also deeply disapprove of people who use others for their own gain, whether it's asking for money, exploiting connections, or taking advantage of someone's kindness.

To be honest, I can be quite discerning when it comes to people. My main dislike is those who use others for their own gain. On a different note, I appreciate you taking the time to read this. It's interesting to consider why someone would be interested in these personal details, but I'm glad you're here. There are some things I've chosen to omit, either because I simply forgot about them or because they're a bit too personal to share.
