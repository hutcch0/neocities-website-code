more about hutcch

This page is clips of my life so you get a background.

I was born in the UK and grew up with a family of four. As a child, I had an Xbox and an old phone, and I was always fascinated by games and computers. Over the years, I upgraded to newer phones like the iPhone 4s, 6s, and eventually the XR. However, it wasn't until 2021 or 2022 that I finally got my own PC.

In school, I was very quiet and shy, and I didn't like talking to people I didn't know. However, I eventually found my own group of friends. Throughout my school years, I often found myself doodling in my notebook instead of focusing on the lessons. As a result, my grades weren't particularly impressive in most subjects. However, I excelled in ICT class, where I earned my highest grade.

Around 2021 or 2022, I became increasingly interested in coding and PC games. This led me to explore game development using Unreal Engine and Unity, although I never fully dedicated myself to it. I also dabbled in woodwork and discovered a passion for creating avatars in Blender. This newfound interest led me to VRChat, where I spent countless hours connecting with people I met both in the game and in real life.

I've always enjoyed reading a wide variety of books. While I used to watch a lot of anime, I've found myself less drawn to it lately. My favorite manga series is Hellsing or darling in the franxx. This interest in manga developed over the past couple of years as I got older and gained the freedom to explore my own interests.

This is just a bit of my life, sharing more about my past and where I'm heading in the future.
